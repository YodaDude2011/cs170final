The Best Movies according to Caden


My top 3 movies

Star Wars: Episode 3

This specific Star Wars movie is my favorite because it showed the downfall of the Jedi and the new era of the Sith. When I was younger, I loved learning about the background lore of the Star Wars, so this concept interested me the most

Pacific Rim

This movie is about giant humanoid mechs fighting Kaiju. I loved the action in this movie.

Midsommar

Midsommar is about a couple who go on a trip to Sweden, but the village is run by a cult. The movie is a little scary and messes with your head. I like This type of thriller movie.
